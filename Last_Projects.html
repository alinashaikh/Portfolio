In my CSP class, we got to do a project on Scratch, where we could make a story or a game with a partner. We designed the game to be a story with a small story in it. In the story, there is a character named Giga who got his "item" stolen, and wants it back...If you want to find out what happens, click the link below! My overall experience working with a partner in CSP was great, I did most of the documenting and he mainly focused on coding.
